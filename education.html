FINKI

The Faculty of Information Sciences and Computer Engineering (FINKI) is a result of the merger of the two largest institutes in the field of informatics and computer technology in Macedonia - the Institute of Informatics (II) at the Faculty of Natural Sciences and Mathematics and the Institute of Computer Engineering and Informatics (ICTI) at Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Information Technologies.
